About Us

CYBERINTEL is a cybersecurity-focused initiative dedicated to proactively identifying compromised infrastructure before it becomes a threat. We investigate leaks found across dark web marketplaces, Telegram channels, stealer logs, and other underground networks.

While our website may be newly launched, our work is anything but. Our team has been working privately within the infosec space, responsibly identifying and reporting risks to organizations, with a zero-tolerance policy for exploitation or misuse. Our goal is simple: prevent breaches before they happen — entirely free of cost.

Our Services

  • Monitoring stealer logs, dark web forums, and underground channels for exposed digital infrastructure
  • Identifying compromised admin panels, sensitive dashboards, and credentials in circulation
  • Notifying companies and organizations with detailed threat insight
  • Providing all disclosures free of charge under responsible disclosure practices
